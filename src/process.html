The People Of Neontribe

Our process is agile and user centered. Divided into three stages — Discovery, Development, Delivery (see below) — it is designed to ensure money and time is not wasted during development.

You can join us at Development, but we'd prefer to collaborate on your Discovery process if possible.

Discovery

  • Personas
  • User stories
  • Prototyping
  • Prioritisation

Discovery starts with people and objectives.

First, we work with you to understand your organisation's objectives.

We create some personas: People who might use the thing we're making.

We think about what these personas want to achieve with this thing.

We build an interactive prototype in paper.

After testing the prototype with users, we prioritise work.

Development

  • Coding
  • Testing
  • Visual design

Our instinct is for agile and iterative development.

The prototype is made manifest in HTML, javascript, sometimes PHP & Python.

Automated tests are run and users enlisted to break the nascent product.

From style guide to interface, a responsive visual design evolves.

Delivery

  • Launch
  • Measurement
  • Improvement

Delivery is not the end of a project.

It's time to release the alpha and see how it gets on.

We keep an eye on its performance and watch to see what works.

Then it's back to “Development” with new user data.
